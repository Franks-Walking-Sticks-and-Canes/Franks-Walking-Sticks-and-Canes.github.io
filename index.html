About

Previsouly known for BBQ Wagon, but is now retired to put his passion towards woodworking. With Frank's Walking Sticks and Canes he can make custom designs that can be for a funtional or the fashionable set pieces.

The Process

Frank uses wood from local businesses and uses his two lasers to engrave custom designs and sometimes finishing with a layer of Epoxy for smoothness.
